Schedlur

A smart tool to generate conflict-free semester schedules based on your priorities and course sections.

Features

  • Handle multiple sections per course
  • Prioritize courses and respect credit limits
  • Generate multiple schedules for comparison
  • Optional credit overload or blocked time slots
