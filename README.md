# schedulr

schedulr is a smart course scheduling tool designed to help students plan their semester efficiently.  

you provide the courses you want to take, assign priorities to each, and input the available sections with their meeting times. schedulr generates multiple feasible schedules that avoid overlapping classes and respect credit limits, giving you several options to choose from. you can also leave specific times free or allow for credit overload if needed! 

the goal is to make semester planning easier, faster, and more flexible, so you can focus on choosing the schedule that best fits your needs!
